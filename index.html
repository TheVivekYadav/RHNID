About Red Hat Academy

Red Hat Academy provides high schools and higher education institutions worldwide with a no-cost curriculum to prepare students for careers in open source. The program provides hands-on instruction, curriculum, labs, performance-based testing, and instructor support.

Our program helps academic institutions keep pace with the demands of industry by delivering resources to help students build knowledge and skills in Linux, cloud, containers, and more.
